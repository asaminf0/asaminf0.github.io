About Me

My name is Atefeh, I'm from Iran and live in Charlotte NC. I have been taking photos about 5 years. I am children and family photographer; nature inspires me in photography. I like to creat unlimited numbers of beautiful dreams and emotions through the camera; that moments, I plunge in to the world of fairy tales. I love the people who take photos of them, also, I have never seen them before, but I live with them through their photos. Life is like a camera, Just focus on what's important and have a good time.
